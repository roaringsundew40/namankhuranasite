Future Martians - FRC 9023

2022 - CURRENT

The Future Martians is a FIRST Robotics Competition team and we a rookie team based in Sammamish, WA. This year, in the 2022-2023 Charged Up Season, we have won the Rookie All Star Award in the PNW Bonney Lake Event, the Rookie High-Seed and the Creativity Award at the PNW Sammamish Event, the Rookie All-Star Award at the PNW District Championship, and the Rookie All-Star Award at the FIRST Houston World Championship in the Newton Division.
